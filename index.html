camel facts

Chameleons have eyes that can swivel.

cicada facts
Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus.
They're true bugs (from the order Hemiptera).

panda facts

Their eyes are different to normal bears.

pigeon facts
Although harmless, they can be quite a nuisance when food is around.
Pigeons, in their natural habitat, or home, live near rocky cliffs.
In this environment, the pigeons will build their nests high on the cliffs, making it challenging for predators to reach them or their eggs.

teddy bear facts
